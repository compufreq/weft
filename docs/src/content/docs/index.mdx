## Why Weft?

If you self-host Weaviate, you get raw APIs and nothing else — the official console targets Weaviate Cloud, and the alternatives are IDE extensions or thin wrappers.

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Zero-config" icon="rocket">
    One container next to your Weaviate. `docker run`, open your browser, done. No account, no
    IDE, no YAML required.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Web-based" icon="laptop">
    Usable by everyone on the team — data QA, ops, PMs — not just people with VS Code.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Weaviate-deep" icon="magnifier">
    Multi-tenancy, hybrid search, schema diff, and node health as first-class features, not
    afterthoughts.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Open source" icon="github">
    AGPL-3.0 licensed, Rust + SolidStart, tested against real Weaviate in CI.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
